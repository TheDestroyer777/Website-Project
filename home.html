    Information:

The STEAM Ohm Contest is the opportunity for young people to show their creativity and innovative ideas. This unique competition challenges young people to work on a project or experiment within one of six current themes during the school year. The highlight of the contest is the final day on May 15, 2025, on which the teams present their experiments or projects to an expert jury.

For more information you can go to the official website.
